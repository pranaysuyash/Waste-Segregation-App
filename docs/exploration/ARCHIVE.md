# Exploration Archive

**Purpose**: Holding ground for topics that have been **killed** or **completed**, so the active [EXPLORATION_TOPICS.md](../EXPLORATION_TOPICS.md) stays the cursor of *current* unknowns without erasing the trail.

**Last Updated**: 2026-05-19

## How to use this file

Move a topic here when it transitions to one of:

- `[KILLED]` — investigation showed it's not worth pursuing, premise was wrong, or it was duplicated by something better.
- `[✓ COMPLETED]` — the exploration produced its deliverable and the result is live in code / docs / decision.

When moving:

1. Cut the topic block out of [../EXPLORATION_TOPICS.md](../EXPLORATION_TOPICS.md) (don't just relabel — keep the active index lean).
2. Paste it under the relevant section below.
3. Append a closing block:
   - **Closed on**: ISO date
   - **Verdict**: `[KILLED]` or `[✓ COMPLETED]`
   - **Why**: one to three sentences. Be honest. "Premise didn't survive contact with X." "Subsumed by topic Y." "Built; see Z." "Re-classified as planning, not research."
   - **Pointer**: link to the artefact, PR, decision doc, or successor topic.
4. If the topic had cross-references in other active topics, update those references to point here.

Do not delete entries from this file. The archive is the record of intellectual honesty about what we thought, why we stopped thinking it, and what replaced it. Future contributors (including future you) need it.
